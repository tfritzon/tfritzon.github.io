Callsign Game – An homage to Rufz and QRQ

When you press the start button, it plays a random callsign. If you enter the correct call, it will play another, one character per minute faster. You can set the start speed, and even change the speed during play using the slider.

Pressing F6 or ESC repeats the call, but only once – just as in Rufz and QRQ.

Callsigns are taken from our log from CQWW SSB 2017 at SK0UX/SJ0X.
